bpa-v27.txt — "Tighten the claim, reconcile the arc, and push toward the L²M empirical lower boundary on H100"

Mission:
Turn BPA v24–v26 into a tight, publication-grade result with one clear central claim, one clear mechanism story, and one clear path toward the L²M empirical lower boundary.

Starting point:
We already have a strong summary result:

Core claim:
- The number of transformer layers requiring high-precision KV protection under mixed-precision KV quantization is bounded by a small constant k*, rather than growing with depth D.
- Observed k* = {2,2,2,0} across D = {24,28,28,32}, spanning 4 models, 2 architectures, and 2 GPUs.
- This yields kv_ratio around 0.28–0.30 and ~3.5x concurrent sequence capacity at <3% PPL cost.
- Qwen exhibits sink-dominance behavior.
- Mistral exhibits uniform robustness with k*=0.

What is missing:
- A single reconciled, canonical benchmark table across versions.
- A precise definition of the "L²M empirical lower boundary" that is careful and not overclaimed.
- One more polished H100 pass focused on confirmatory rather than exploratory experiments.
- A trimmed, paper-ready technical narrative.

This version is not for broad exploration. It is for tightening, reconciling, confirming, and packaging.

PHASE 1 — Define the central publishable claim precisely (2–4h)
========================================================
Goal:
Make the main result maximally sharp and defensible.

Tasks:
1.1) State the central claim in final form:
- k* remains O(1) over the tested depth range under mixed-precision KV quantization at ε=3%.
- This is supported empirically, not asymptotically proven.
- Distinguish clearly between:
  - empirical support over tested D
  - theoretical condition under which O(1) would continue

1.2) Define the "L²M empirical lower boundary" carefully:
This is NOT the information-theoretic lower bound itself.
Define it as:
- the lowest empirically achieved effective KV ratio under a fixed quality tolerance ε
- within the tested family of mixed-precision/tiering mechanisms
- after accounting for structural metadata overhead
- and under a defined model, context, and ranking procedure

Then explain the connection:
- If k* remains bounded while D increases, the protected fraction k*/D goes to zero
- therefore effective KV ratio approaches the quantization/storage floor
- this is an empirical route toward the L²M-style lower-bound story, not the lower bound itself

1.3) Produce one formal "claim box" and one "limitations box" for the report.
No fluff.
No hype.
No "this proves transformers are optimal."
Be precise.

Deliverable:
- docs/bpa_v27_claim_and_scope.md
